Transmission

One developer. Many worlds.

Agile Conqueror Company is an indie mobile app studio built around curiosity, craft, and the joy of creating small digital worlds. From sci-fi companion tools and interactive RPG ideas to cat quizzes, geography puzzles, kids' learning, and everyday utilities — every app is made with the same goal: to become a little better with every release.

This isn't a corporate fleet. It's a personal workshop at the edge of the final frontier — serious enough for command-deck companions, warm enough for cats and kids, and handcrafted with care.
