Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Agile Conqueror Company ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our mobile applications and this website (cevik10.github.io).

Overview

Agile Conqueror Company is an independent mobile app studio. Privacy practices may vary slightly between individual apps. This policy provides a general overview. App-specific details may also appear within each application's settings or dedicated policy page.

Information We Collect

Many of our apps are designed to work primarily on your device. Depending on the app, we may handle:

  • App usage data — such as preferences, progress, or settings stored locally on your device.
  • Purchase information — processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play); we do not receive your full payment details.
  • Support communications — if you email us, we receive the information you choose to send.
  • Analytics or advertising data — some apps may use third-party services (e.g., advertising networks). Details will be disclosed in the relevant app's settings where applicable.

Information We Do Not Collect

Unless explicitly stated in an individual app's documentation, we do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information such as government IDs, financial account numbers, or precise location data for identification purposes.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold. Contact us to exercise these rights.
